Strategic Product Placement Analysis

Unveiling Sales Impact with Tableau Visualization

Project Overview

This project investigates the relationship between product positioning, sales performance, and consumer behavior. By using Tableau for data visualization, we aim to identify how product placement strategies influence customer preferences and sales, enabling companies to optimize marketing efforts and increase revenue.

Business Scenario

A retail company wants to understand the impact of product positioning on its sales. With data on sales, product placement, and consumer demographics, they seek insights into effective strategies for boosting visibility and conversions.

Use Case Scenarios

  • Scenario 1 - Film and TV Production: Analyze product placements in scenes or episodes to negotiate brand deals and maximize audience engagement.
  • Scenario 2 - Retail & Consumer Goods: Track product performance in various store/website locations to boost visibility and conversions.
  • Scenario 3 - Advertising Agencies: Evaluate product placements in media to measure ROI, enhance targeting, and improve campaign effectiveness.
